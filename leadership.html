Our Leadership

The Visionaries
Behind the Vision

Great enterprises are built by extraordinary people. Our promoters bring a rare combination of global perspective, local expertise, and an unwavering commitment to excellence that defines every TLBPS endeavour.

Promoter

Mr. Terence Larue Bolden

Mr. Terence Larue Bolden is a co-promoter of TLBPS Infratech Developers Pvt. Ltd. He brings a strong commitment to delivering sustainable, high-value real estate and infrastructure development projects across Central and South India.

With a focus on innovation, transparency, and sustainability, Mr. Bolden is instrumental in driving TLBPS's vision for eco-conscious development — from the flagship hospitality project in Mysore to the upcoming residential communities in Bangalore and Indore.

Promoter

Mr. Praneet Kishore Solanki

Mr. Praneet Kishore Solanki is a co-promoter of TLBPS Infratech Developers Pvt. Ltd. He plays a key role in shaping the company's approach to real estate and urban development across Central and South India.

His dedication to building sustainable, functional, and community-oriented spaces is at the heart of TLBPS's mission. Mr. Solanki is actively involved in steering the residential developments planned for Bangalore and Indore, as well as the flagship eco-luxury hospitality project in Mysore.

Together, we bring the world to India and India to the world. Our partnership is built on complementary strengths, shared values, and an unshakeable belief that the best is yet to be built — Saath mein, sab sambhav hai.
