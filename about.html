About

Who We Are

Innovation. Transparency.
Sustainability.

At TLBPS Infratech Developers Pvt. Ltd., we are a future-focused infrastructure development company committed to delivering excellence in real estate and urban development across Central and South India.

With a strong foundation of innovation, transparency, and sustainability, we are proud to announce our entry into the hospitality sector with a flagship project in Mysore, followed by a series of residential developments in Bangalore and Indore.

As a trusted name in the real estate and infrastructure landscape, our goal is simple yet powerful: to create spaces that are not only beautiful but sustainable, functional, and community-oriented.

What Makes Us Different

Development + Oversight + Sustainability

Most firms do one thing. We integrate all three into a seamless value chain that delivers superior outcomes for investors, communities, and the environment.

From concept to completion, we bring institutional-grade rigour to every phase of development. Our projects are not just built — they are meticulously engineered for lasting value.
