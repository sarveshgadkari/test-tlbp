Company Profile

TLBPS Infratech Developers Private Limited is a diversified real estate and infrastructure development company focused on creating high-quality residential, commercial, hospitality, and mixed-use developments across Central and South India.

The company combines strategic land acquisition, project development expertise, and asset management capabilities to create long-term value for stakeholders and investors.

Strategic Objectives

Expand presence across Central and South India.

Build a diversified real estate portfolio.

Develop approximately 500,000 sq. ft. of real estate assets within the initial growth phase.

Establish strategic partnerships with investors, contractors, hospitality operators, and financial institutions.

Create sustainable, income-generating real estate assets with long-term value appreciation.

ESG Framework

Environmental, Social
and Governance Commitment

TLBPS is committed to developing environmentally responsible, socially beneficial, and ethically governed real estate assets that create long-term value for investors, customers, communities, and stakeholders.

Environmental Responsibility

Energy-efficient building design, natural lighting and ventilation, environmentally friendly and locally sourced materials where feasible, and green building practices across project types.

Rainwater harvesting, wastewater treatment and recycling, water-efficient plumbing fixtures, LED lighting, solar energy solutions, smart energy monitoring, EV charging, construction waste segregation, and preservation of green spaces.

Social Responsibility

Employment opportunities during construction and operation, engagement with local communities, support for local suppliers and contractors, zero-tolerance safety practices, regular safety training, equal opportunity employment, professional development, and transparent customer communication.

Governance

Compliance with applicable laws, transparent financial reporting, anti-bribery and anti-corruption standards, board oversight, risk management, internal review, and open communication with investors and partners.

Long-Term ESG Targets (2030)

Environmental Targets

25% reduction in project energy consumption through efficiency measures.

Solar power integration across major developments.

100% rainwater harvesting implementation in new projects.

Social Targets

Generate 500+ direct and indirect employment opportunities and support local community development initiatives in operating regions.

Governance Targets

Maintain full regulatory compliance across all projects.

Establish ESG reporting as part of annual corporate disclosures.

ESG Vision

To develop sustainable real estate assets that enhance communities, preserve environmental resources, and deliver long-term value for stakeholders.
