Frequently Asked

Questions
& Answers

What defines the TLBPS approach?

TLBPS combines project development, asset management, and ESG-minded planning to create value-driven real estate assets across multiple segments.

How can I learn more about the company?

Review the funding fact sheet and contact the team through the website for company information, business discussions, or partnership enquiries.

What is the sustainability focus?

The company’s ESG framework emphasizes energy efficiency, rainwater harvesting, wastewater treatment, waste management, green spaces, safety, transparency, and compliance.

What are the company’s long-term targets?

Targets include 25% lower project energy consumption, solar integration across major developments, 100% rainwater harvesting in new projects, 500+ employment opportunities, and annual ESG disclosure.

What business segments does TLBPS cover?

Residential development, commercial development, hospitality and leisure, industrial and infrastructure, and asset management.
